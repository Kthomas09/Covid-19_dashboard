About

These dashboards are designed for everyone to understand the progression of Covid-19. With the help of Johns Hopkins Coronavirus Resource Center, and data scraped from Data.World, we will finally understand the path this disease has taken throughout the United States of America. This virus was devastating early, and as the holiday season is upon us we are seeing the same horror as at the beginning of 2020. However, because of the dedicated medical professionals, risking their health and the health of their families, we are beginning to see signs of hope. As of Dec. 14th, Pfizer has officially begun to distribute their vaccines to those fighting on the front lines. These medical professionals are also becoming more proficient in treating this virus. We have seen a steady decrease in the mortality rate for this disease, and with a vaccine sooon on the way, we can only hope that we will begin to adjust to this new normal. We will never be able to bring back our loved ones, but with their dedication, perhaps we can save many more for our future. This data project is dedicated to our front-line workers who are always there for us! We Thank You!
